About Us

Welcome to Buns N' Roses

For over 25 years, Buns N' Roses has been a cherished part of our community, delivering freshly baked goods crafted with love and passion. From our humble beginnings as a small family-owned bakery, we have grown into a name synonymous with quality and warmth.

Our commitment to using the finest ingredients and traditional recipes has earned us a loyal following. We specialize in everything from delectable cakes and cookies to artisan breads and pastries that make every occasion special.

In 2025, we embarked on a new chapter by moving online, bringing the same dedication and care that has defined us for decades to a broader audience. Now, you can explore our menu, place custom orders, and discover the magic of Buns N' Roses from the comfort of your home.

Thank you for supporting us on this journey. Here's to many more years of sweet moments and cherished memories!
